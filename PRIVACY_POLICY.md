Display Control+ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-21

This Privacy Policy explains how Display Control+ handles information.

1. Data Collection
Display Control+ is a local desktop application. It is designed to run on your device and store runtime data locally (for example, configuration and logs).

2. Local Data Storage
The app may store local files such as:
- configuration settings;
- log files;
- process lock/state files.
These files are used to provide app functionality and diagnostics.

3. Personal Data
Display Control+ does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal data through a hosted service operated by Knight Logics.

4. Network Activity
Display Control+ does not require ongoing cloud connectivity for core features. If future versions include online features, this policy may be updated.

5. Third-Party Dependencies
The app may use third-party libraries. Their behavior is governed by their respective licenses and documentation.

6. Data Security
You are responsible for securing your own device and user account. Knight Logics recommends standard endpoint protections and regular system updates.

7. Changes to this Policy
Knight Logics may update this policy from time to time. The effective date will reflect the latest revision.

8. Contact
For privacy questions, contact Knight Logics through the official website.
